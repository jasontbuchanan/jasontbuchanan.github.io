Jason Thorpe Buchanan

Jason Thorpe Buchanan is currently in his senior year at San Jose State University, where he studies composition with Dr. Pablo Furman. In addition, he previously studied under Prof. Charles "Gus" Gustavson at the College of San Mateo. Current compositional projects include an independently produced rock album, an operetta, a song cycle, a piece for horn & electronics, sound design and foley for film and an original score plus arrangements for film.
